This heatmap shows how often is each square of the board occupied. The darker the square, the more often players set a piece onto it. Use the buttons to choose the range of ELO used as input data.

Notice how the center of the board is more prized by higher level players than beginners. It is indeed often missed by new players: you should always try to control the center of the board. Center control yields greater influence over the board and a greater mobility for your pieces.

Tips: Hover over a square to see the occupation percentation.
